ABOUT REMA

Born Divine Ikubor, who is popularly known as Rema – is a rising star in the music industry who has taken the world by storm with his distinct blend of Afrobeats, trap, and R&B. Hailing from Nigeria, Rema has captured the hearts of millions with his soulful voice and charismatic stage presence. During the early stage of his career in 2019, his sound got tagged as an ‘Indian’ sound. Netizens said he sounds like the Indians and will not go far with the style. In 2023, Rema went on a tour in India and shut down the shows.
